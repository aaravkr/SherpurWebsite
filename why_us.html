Why
Sherpur

Prime
Location

For any industry location is important but quick and efficient access to modern transportation is fundamental and cost efficient.

Strategically located in Amta, Mouza : Serpur, which is in close proximity to the city of Kolkata, SIP offers you to benefit from an outstanding low-cost access to adjacent markets through road, railway, sea and airways thus easily connecting you to the adjacent states and counties.(Distance note, railway routes and bus routes).

Power
Availability

-The industrial power feeders lines up to the Plot. The Park carries a network for power distribution to ensure reliable transmission of electricity to all the sites within the park as per the standards laid by West Bengal Electricity Board

Modern
Roads

Roads are designed to carry heavy and oversized cargo. Main roads are 35 Ft wide and inter-connected for movement with landscaped traffic medians, storm water drains, efficient visual elements, lighting and signage.

Sewage
Treatment

A common sewage treatment facility with compliance to all the West bengal Pollution Control Board directives is developed within the park to service the sewage discharged by the occupants of the park

Ambience

Landscaped traffic medians, storm water drains, efficient visual elements, lighting and signage systems are provided to enable a clutter free and smooth movement of people and vehicles.

Water
Availability

Ground water which richly endows the site, will be pumped and stored in elevated and ground-level reservoirs. Running across 3048 metres, the Park’s water distribution network — designed for industrial use — will be supplied by Elevated Storage Reservoir (ESR) totaling to 250 KLD.

SECURITY, FIRE-SAFETY &
POLLUTION CONTROL

SIP believes that an effective security and fire-safety system is necessary in providing an uninterrupted growth to your blooming industry. Thus, we provide a 24x7 security service with CCTV surveillance and in-house fire-safety and extinguisher setups.
