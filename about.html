About
Sherpur

Overview

Sherpur Industrial Park was formed in 2017 with the aim to extend support, promote industrialization and to address to the challenges of companies grappling with building and management of industrial and warehousing spaces in a large, diverse and geographically distributed market like India. We help national and multinational companies and clients with specific mandate to acquire land and then proceed to develop an industrial campus with a “pro-business regulatory environment.

About

SIP is committed to bringing grade A quality in industrial, light manufacturing and warehousing spaces in proximity to leading spaces and industrial centers in West Bengal.

Built on land parcel of 300 Bighas, it is strategically located in Amta, Mouza which lies close to the city of Kolkata thus, providing access to adjoining countries and a dozen states. Our world-class experienced team fully masters in planning how to specifically cater to industrial and logistic traffic movement and offers you to benefit from an industry friendly and hassle-free business environment.

Sherpur industrial park will boast of host of special amenities such as reliable, sustainable and state-of-the art business estates with eco-friendly infrastructure and sound environmental policies. For companies wishing to expand their operations and workspace we offer a complete “build to suit” space ready to augment existing operations.
